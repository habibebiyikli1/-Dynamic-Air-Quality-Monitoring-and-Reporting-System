DYNAMIC AIR QUALITY MONITORING AND REPORTING SYSTEM

Air pollution is defined by various international organizations as the cancer of the 20th century and continues to take place in the first century of problems in the world in the 21st century. Air pollution and environmental health problems are a big problem created by humanity, which is trying to improve development and living standards. And as a result, he is influenced again. No resource in our world, including renewable resources, is unlimited. On the contrary, the contamination of even the most abundant air shows us that our resources are scarce. And it is a meaningful warning that the problems that may arise if they are not consciously used can threaten our future.

Along with the industrial revolution, the growth of cities around the world is increasing as a result of the increase in vehicle use, rapid industrialization and corresponding shortcomings in planning and environmental regulations. Air pollution has led to health and environmental problems in many countries. Efforts to create a sustainable action plan, to produce reliable and acceptable data for pollutant concentrations, resources and impacts are now essential to our world and our health. Especially in large cities with dense population, comprehensive air quality management plans and effective control measures need to be developed. Determining the sources of air quality monitoring, pollution sources and distribution is very important for developing appropriate control strategies and controlling the effectiveness of these strategies.

The Dynamic Air Quality Monitoring and Reporting System project serves exactly this purpose. This system combines hardware and software together. First of all, it transfers the data of the gases required for air quality measurement to the computer with the help of sensors. It stores the measured data through an application. On the other hand, it presents the data stored on a website designed for reporting and analysis as dynamic and time stamped. The analysis of this data can be presented to the enthusiast at the simplest level, creating awareness and harmonizing with other monitoring systems and providing optimum benefit from the data.
